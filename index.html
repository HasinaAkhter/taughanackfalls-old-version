Wel-come to a scenic spot in Ithaca

Ithaca is a city in the popular Finger Lakes region in New York and the Finger Lakes is a region of a group of 11 long, narrow, roughly north-south lakes in Central New York of USA. The lakes' shapes reminded early map-makers of human fingers and this region is a popular attraction site for tourists.

Taughannock Falls is part of Taughannock State Park located between the Village of Trumansburg and the city of Ithaca. Its a popular tourists attraction site in Ithaca.

The falls cataract has a drop of 215 feet (66 meters), and is one of the highest east of the Rocky Mountains.Taughannock State Park also features hiking and nature trails, ice-skating, sledding,tent and trailer sites,cabins, picnic areas, beach swimming, fishing, playground areas, cross-country skiing and an annual summer concert series.

You can also explore other interesting area attractions which are from ten minutes to an hour drive from the falls such as: Buttermilk Falls, Cornell University, Stewart State Park, BB Lake Cornell, Robert Treman State Park, Cayuga Lake etc.

Hours of Operation

Park is open throughout the year from dawn to dusk. For more details, please call the park.
